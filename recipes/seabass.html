Sea bass with sizzled ginger, chilli & spring onions

Ingredients

  1. 2x sea bass fillets, about 140g/5oz each, skin on and scaled
  2. 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. knob of ginger, peeled and shredded into matchsticks
  4. 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
  5. 1.5 fat, fresh red chillies deseeded and thinly shredded
  6. bunch spring onion, shredded long-ways
  7. 0.5 tbsp soy sauce

Method

STEP 1: Season sea bass fillets with salt and pepper, then slash the skin 3 times.

STEP 2: Heat a heavy-based frying pan and add some oil.

STEP 3: Once hot, fry the sea bass fillets, skin-side down, for 5 mins or until the skin is very crisp and golden. The fish will be almost cooked through.

STEP 4: Turn over, cook for another 30 seconds - 1 minute, then transfer to a serving plate and keep warm. Youâ€™ll need to fry the sea bass fillets in 2 batches.

STEP 5: Heat some oil, then fry the large knob of peeled ginger, cut into matchsticks, 1 thinly sliced garlic clove and 1.5 thinly shredded red chillies for about 2 mins until golden.

STEP 6: Take off the heat and toss in the bunch of shredded spring onions. Splash the fish with 1 tbsp soy sauce and spoon over the contents of the pan.

Here endeth the recipe.
